English Summary

Ken Kurose is a Japan-based independent consultant specializing in AI governance, data protection, and cybersecurity. Certified in CISSP, CEH, and AIGP (AI Governance Professional), he supports public and private organizations with GDPR/AI Act compliance, privacy risk assessment (e.g., DPIA), and the implementation of ISO/IEC 42001 or NIST AI RMF frameworks. He is also active as a guest lecturer at major Japanese universities and contributes to industry bodies and policy dialogues.
